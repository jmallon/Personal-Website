About Me


I am currently researching how weather affects the stopover behavior of migrant turkey vultures. I use this understanding of their response to weather to quantify how much energy vultures require to migrate and to stopover, and predict how climate change will affect their survival and migration performance!

Research Interests

  • Conservation
  • Data Science
